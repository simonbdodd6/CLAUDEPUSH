Pitchero — The All-in-One Sports Club Website Builder

Pitchero helps over 10,000 clubs manage their club website, membership, payments and communications.

Pricing:
- Starter: Free (website only, up to 25 members)
- Standard: £25/month (full features, unlimited members)
- Premium: £45/month (advanced stats, custom domain)

Features:
- Club website builder
- Member management
- Match scheduling and results
- Payment collection (membership fees, match fees)
- Team messaging
- Stats and player profiles
- Mobile app (iOS + Android)

Target: Grassroots UK sports clubs across all sports (football, rugby, cricket, hockey)

Weaknesses noted from user reviews:
- Not rugby-specific — generic across all sports
- App feels dated and slow on older phones
- Customer support response time poor
- No availability-first workflow — coaches have to chase players
- Push notifications unreliable on Android
