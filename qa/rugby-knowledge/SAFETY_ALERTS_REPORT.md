### 🛡️ World Rugby Law Amendment — Tackle Height Directive
_31 Dec 2023 · Source: law:example-law-update.txt_

World Rugby Law Amendment — Tackle Height Directive Effective: 1 January 2024 SUMMARY FOR COACHES AND REFEREES World Rugby has implemented a new directive on tackle height across all levels of the game. The directive lowers the legal tackle height and introduces clearer guidance on what constitutes dangerous play. TACKLE HEIGHT The legal tackle height is now defined as at or below the line of the hips.
